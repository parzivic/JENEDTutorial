About JenEd

At JenEd Tutorial, led by Prof. Jeniel, we believe that every child can learn, grow, and succeed with the right guidance and support. Our tutorial center is dedicated to helping students build strong foundations in essential skills they need to excel in school and in life.

We provide a safe, supportive, and encouraging learning environment where students feel comfortable to ask questions, make mistakes, and improve step by step.
